About

Turkish WebVectors is a tool to compute semantic relations between words in Turkish on the web, making it easy to demonstrate their abilities to general public. It is based on the toolkit WebVectors Semantic vectors reflect meaning based on word co-occurrence distribution in the training corpus (huge amounts of raw linguistic data).
